Full stack developer

A full stack developer has the functional knowledge and skills to turn a client's entire work concept into a finished product. A full stack developer must have knowledge in both the front end and back end, different areas of development.

A front-end developer builds the visible parts of applications that users see and interact with. A back-end developer, on the other hand, is responsible for building the parts that are not visible to the user, which include the basic operating logic, the admin interface, the web server, and database management.

UI/UX Design

User experience and interface (UI/UX) design is the design of the process that a person experiences when interacting with a product. UX design focuses on the interaction between the human user and the product.

It aims to provide a designed user experience for the visitor. If the user feels while visiting the website that it is easy to use, easy to understand and logically structured, then the ux designer has done a good job.

Conversion Optimized Website

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) is the practice of increasing the percentage of users who take a desired action on your website. The desired actions, also known as conversions, can be purchasing a product, filling out a form, contacting a customer, or using a service.

Successful CRO strategies aim to increase conversions by focusing on user experience and optimized content.
